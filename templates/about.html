Philosophy

Immersion is the best way to learn a language.

That said, if you've ever tried to learn a language via immersion, you know there are a few things that are frustrating about it. To list a few:
  1. The first time you're in a situation, you often are underprepared for the vocabulary it requires
  2. When you come across new words, you often need many repetitions to actually learn them, so you may get them wrong in real life even after you’ve heard them once or twice
  3. It's easy to forget words that you haven't used for a while
While ultimately your growing brain is the powerhouse for your learning, this tool is designed to help make your immersion as effective as possible by making it easy for you to
  1. Prepare for upcoming scenarios
  2. Save and review new words you come across
  3. Keep all of your vocabulary fresh

A few suggestions how to use this app

Keep all of your vocabulary fresh

Quiz yourself on all the words you’ve saved, and keep track of progress over time! The more you answer a word correctly, the less often you will see it over time, and vice versa.

Prepare for upcoming scenarios

Look up and save specific words
Look up and save related words
Practice chatting
Hit the chat icon and explore yourself!
Save words from sentences
You can look up articles and past them in or type your own sentences. Add them in either your home language or your learning language!

Save and review new words you come across

While immersing or after the fact, save what’s new to you, and study it later. And more! These are just a few ideas for how to use these tools. You know what works for your learning - do whatever is most useful for you!
